## Content

Menus help users find content and use features. Labels on menus should give users a strong indication of what they can find behind them.

<Use>
	<Use.Do>
		<ul>
			<li>Use meaningful labels that clearly describe what contained within menus.</li>
			<li>
				Choose descriptive words and phrases that your users relate to, even if the words sound
				boring. Itâ€™s essential that your navigational labels can be easily understood.
			</li>
		</ul>
	</Use.Do>
	<Use.Dont>
		<ul>
			<li>
				Avoid made-up terms. For example, according to the leader in research-based user experience NN/g Nielsen Norman Group "About Us" proved to be better than "Company Experience" because it is a more-common phrase.
			</li>
		</ul>
	</Use.Dont>
</Use>
